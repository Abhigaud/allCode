Historical Places of India

Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in the world. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is a symbol of love.

Red Fort, Delhi

The Red Fort is a historic fort in the city of Delhi. It served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors for nearly 200 years.

Hampi, Karnataka

Hampi is an ancient village known for its ruined temples and monuments from the Vijayanagara Empire.
