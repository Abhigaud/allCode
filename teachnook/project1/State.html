Andhra Pradesh

The Tirumala Venkateswara Temple in Andhra Pradesh is one of the richest and most visited temples in the world.

Tawang Monastery is the largest monastery in India, situated in the scenic hills of Arunachal Pradesh.

Assam

Kamakhya Temple in Assam is one of the most revered Shakti Peethas, located in Guwahati.

Bihar

The Mahabodhi Temple in Bihar is a UNESCO World Heritage site where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment.

Chhattisgarh

Chitrakote Waterfall, often called the "Niagara Falls of India," is a stunning attraction in Chhattisgarh.

Goa

The Basilica of Bom Jesus in Goa is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a significant religious landmark.

Gujarat

Somnath Temple in Gujarat is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and a sacred pilgrimage site.

Haryana

Brahma Sarovar in Haryana is an ancient water tank in Kurukshetra, linked to Hindu mythology.

Himachal Pradesh

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is a popular hill station known for its colonial architecture.

Jharkhand

Baidyanath Temple in Jharkhand is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and a revered Hindu shrine.

Karnataka

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage site in Karnataka, showcases the grandeur of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Kerala

Kerala's Alleppey is famous for its backwaters and luxurious houseboat cruises.

Madhya Pradesh

Khajuraho Temples in Madhya Pradesh are UNESCO World Heritage sites known for their intricate carvings.

Manipur

Meghalaya

Living Root Bridges in Meghalaya are handmade bridges grown from tree roots over decades.

Mizoram

Vantawng Falls, the highest waterfall in Mizoram, is a breathtaking natural wonder.

Kohima War Cemetery in Nagaland is a memorial for soldiers who fought in World War II.

Odisha

The Konark Sun Temple in Odisha is a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its stunning architecture.

Punjab

The Golden Temple in Punjab is the holiest site of Sikhism, located in Amritsar.

Rajasthan

Hawa Mahal in Rajasthan is an iconic palace with a unique honeycomb structure in Jaipur.

Sikkim

Tsomgo Lake in Sikkim is a high-altitude glacial lake, offering breathtaking views.

Telangana

Charminar in Telangana is an iconic historical monument located in Hyderabad.

Uttar Pradesh

Taj Mahal in Uttar Pradesh is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Uttarakhand

Kedarnath Temple in Uttarakhand is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.

West Bengal

Victoria Memorial in West Bengal is a grand marble structure dedicated to Queen Victoria.
